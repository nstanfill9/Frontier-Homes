Steve founded Frontier Homes in 2004 with the vision of creating a home building empire. Steve knew that he wanted to get involved in the custom home business, but he didn't quite know how. Instead of working for one of the established building companies in West Tennessee, Steve decided that he wanted to go to work for himself. Frontier Homes was then created.

Adam has been with Frontier Homes as the only other full time employee since it was founded in 2004. Steve knew that he needed help when he founded Frontier Homes, so Adam joined the home building effort. Adam has been building custom homes with Steve for over 16 years, making him one of the most experienced custom home builders in West Tennessee.
