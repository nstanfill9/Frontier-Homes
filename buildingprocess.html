Before the main construction of the house is built, the land where the house will be built must be prepped properly. The ground must be extremely level to insure the integrity of the house.
After the ground has been broken, the structure, or skeleton, of the house is ready to go up. The frame of the house makes sure that the house will stand for many years.
Once the frame of the house is built, the detail work can begin. The dry wall is put up, which divides the house into seperate rooms. The windows are created in the part of the process as well.
>

The last step in the building process is making the house look like a home. Everything is being painted and finishing touches are put on. After this step is complete, the new homeowner will be able to move in!
